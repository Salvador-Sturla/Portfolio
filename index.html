The Human Method

The Human Method is a first person horror game. You play as Jack, the son of a deceased tycoon who founded an automaton development company. The new CEO asks for your help at the factory to fix a bug in the AI software, but things go south when the power is cut off and the automatons start murdering everyone.

  • Roles: Lead Game Designer & Level Designer.
  • Main tasks: Design and document all the components to deliver a terrifying experience through Mechanics, Objects, AI, Enemies, Sound, Pacing and Levels.
  • Tools: Unreal Engine 5, Google Docs, Google Sheets, Hack And Plan, Blueprints.
Itch.io: The Human Method

Zupay: Shadows Of Independence

They messed with your homeland. A violent demon saved you from death and now pushes you to get revenge. In this Hack and Slash, explore a revolutionary land, merge with the environment, and consume your enemies' fear to unleash the Zupay.
